Hi, I'm Karthikeyan 👋

I'm a Computer Science and Engineering student specializing in IoT, currently in my B.Tech program. I'm drawn to the intersection of software engineering and connected systems — where clean code meets the physical world.

Java is my primary language right now, and I'm actively studying Data Structures and Algorithms to build the kind of problem-solving instincts that matter in engineering interviews and on the job.

Alongside academics, I've been exploring web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), building small desktop tools with Python, and researching IoT architectures and Digital Twin technology as part of my specialization.

What drives me

I believe the best engineers are perpetual learners — people who stay curious, ship real things, and aren't afraid to start from scratch when they realize there's a better way. That's how I try to approach every project and every concept I pick up.

My long-term goal is to work as a software engineer building systems that actually matter — whether that's a backend service handling millions of requests or an IoT platform connecting industrial infrastructure.

I'm particularly excited about the convergence of IoT and cloud computing — where edge devices talk to distributed systems in real time. That's the kind of engineering challenge I want to be solving a few years from now.
